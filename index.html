WORLD ON FIRE

  • Social Media
  • Design & Layout
  • Illustration

The world on fire promotional advertisement was created for a fictitious Netflix film named "World on Fire". Based off a fan made story, the film centers around a man who was sent to space and must watch as the world below ends. Enveloped in blistering flames and utter destruction, he must find a way to survive without the help from those on earth. The design was straight forward in order to provide context clues - an astronaut floating in space with the reflection of the apocolyptic world in his helmet. Morse code was added to further push the direction of a stranded/helpless man in space.
